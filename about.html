about survival

Working toward any goal can be a difficult process, but if you’re not sure where to start or what to do to make your goal happen, accomplishing that goal becomes infinitely harder. For those of us who wouldn’t call ourselves experts within the realm of survival, but who really are serious about wanting to grow our skills to be better overall survivalists, figuring out which steps to take to reach our goal can be more than a little daunting. Getting better at wilderness survival, bushcraft, and urban survival techniques takes quite a bit of time, and besides that time, you’ll need some sound direction to get you there. Without direction, you likely won’t get very far with your goal, or improve on your skills very quickly.
